ABOUT JLUG-JEC

JLUG JEC is one of the most renowned and official societies of Jabalpur Engineering College, Jabalpur. The society has conducted several workshops which covered a few concrete and significant areas viz AutoCAD, Linux, WordPress, Web Development and Robotics, providing certification from eminent institutions, like IIT Bombay, the importance of which can't be explained merely in words. We're all very aware of the fact and the significance of these certificates during internships, job interviews and placements. Annually, students spend thousands of ruppees, traversing long distances, to attend workshops like Linux, AutoCAD, Web Development and Robotics. JLUG JEC on the other hand, made this entirely free for the students, granting the sum of absolute zero from its students.
